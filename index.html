TREATMENTS AND CLASSES

REIKI

60-90 MIN SESSIONS

Reiki (pronounced "ray-key") is a Japanese technique for stress reduction, relaxation and also promotes healing. A simple and powerful modality.

It was discovered by Mikao Usui in March 1922. Reiki is received by “laying on hands”. By softly touching the body and the chakras the Chi ( Universal ) Energy heals and reconnects our mind, body and heart. It takes us to our inner wisdom and divine/love with in. Reiki sessions are transformative, relieving emotional and physical pain, depression, anxiety, etc.

Because Reiki is guided by spiritual consciousness, it can never harm.  It always knows what a person needs and will adjust itself to create the effect that is required.

RUNNING THE BARS ( Access Conscious )

60-75 MIN SESSIONS

The Bars consist of 32 points on your head, that while laying down are softly touched. Touching this points, gently release the electromagnetic charge of what holds the repressed , repeating, thoughts, feelings, ideas, beliefs, points of view and emotions stored in your body creating more space. This is an opportunity to let go, receive and open to NEW choices and possibilities.

It gives a sense of total ease and life becomes much easier.

BREATHING CLASSES

60 MIN CLASSES

Breathing is the most important thing we do and we do it automatically . We can be without food and without water a few days, but we have to breath and require oxygen to live. We tend to breath automatically and usually we don’t know how we breath. Do we breath deep? Shallow? Fast? Slow?

In this class you will learn different Breathing techniques and their effect in our health and mind. Our breath can balance our mind, help with digestion, relaxation, cleanse the body, reduce stress, detoxifies and invigorates the mind.

Did you know that with 1-2 minutes of Conscious Breath you can feel much better ?

MEDITATION CLASSES

60 MIN CLASSES

Meditation is a mind and body practice that has been practiced for thousand of years in many cultures around the world. It has many benefits in our mind and body: encourages calmness and physical relaxation, improving psychological balance, coping with illness, reducing stress, inner peace and enhancing overall health and well-being.

There are many types of meditation.

Through the practice and learning of different meditation techniques we connect with our Inner wisdom and love.
